# GTM Frameworks

## ORB Framework (Owned, Rented, Borrowed)

| Type | Examples | Control | Risk | Best for |
|------|----------|---------|------|----------|
| Owned | Email, blog, community | Full | Slow growth | Foundation |
| Rented | Social media, Reddit, stores | Algorithm | Platform changes | Reach |
| Borrowed | Podcasts, guest posts, collabs | Negotiated | One-time | Credibility |

**Rule:** All rented/borrowed efforts must funnel into owned channels.

## 5-Phase Launch

| Phase | Duration | Goal | Audience |
|-------|----------|------|----------|
| Internal | 1-2 weeks | Validate functionality | Friendly users |
| Alpha | 2-3 weeks | First external validation | Waitlist |
| Beta | 3-4 weeks | Build buzz | Influencers + testers |
| Early Access | 2-3 weeks | Scale + feedback | Waitlist batch |
| Full Launch | 1 day + post | Max visibility | Everyone |

## Product Hunt Strategy

**Pre-launch (2-4 weeks before):**
- Build relationships with influential supporters
- Optimize listing: tagline, visuals, demo video
- Study successful launches in your category
- Prepare team for all-day engagement

**Launch day:**
- Respond to every comment in real-time
- Answer questions, spark discussions
- Encourage existing audience to engage
- Direct traffic to your site (capture signups)

**Post-launch:**
- Follow up with everyone who engaged
- Convert PH traffic into email signups
- Continue momentum with post-launch content
