# Channel Strategy

## Owned (high priority — compound over time)

| Channel | Best for | Frequency | Launch role |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Email | Direct updates, conversions | Weekly/biweekly | Primary announcement |
| Blog | SEO, authority, education | 1-2x/week | Deep dive post |
| Community | Engagement, feedback | Daily | Early access + buzz |
| Website | Conversion | Always live | Landing page |

## Rented (medium priority — algorithm-dependent)

| Channel | Best for | Launch tactic |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Twitter/X | Real-time buzz | Threads + engagement |
| LinkedIn | B2B/thought leadership | Long-form posts |
| Reddit | Community feedback | Relevant subreddits |
| Product Hunt | Early adopters | Full listing |

## Borrowed (low priority — high value)

| Channel | Best for | Launch tactic |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Podcasts | Credibility | Guest appearances |
| Guest posts | SEO + audience | Industry blogs |
| Influencers | Scale | Reviews + mentions |
| Webinars | Education | Co-marketing |
