# Launch Timeline Template

## Major Launch (4-6 weeks pre)

### Week -6 to -5: Foundation
- [ ] Product positioning finalized
- [ ] Landing page designed
- [ ] Email waitlist created
- [ ] Social profiles optimized
- [ ] Launch date locked

### Week -4 to -3: Asset Creation
- [ ] Launch video (showcase)
- [ ] Social content batch (social-copy)
- [ ] Email sequence drafted (email-drip)
- [ ] Blog post written
- [ ] Product Hunt listing prepared

### Week -2 to -1: Warm-up
- [ ] Teaser posts on social
- [ ] Influencer outreach (borrowed channels)
- [ ] Waitlist emails sent
- [ ] In-app announcement prepared
- [ ] Analytics tracking verified

### Week 0: Launch Day
- [ ] Email blast sent (morning)
- [ ] Blog post published
- [ ] Social posts go live (scheduled)
- [ ] Product Hunt listing live
- [ ] In-app announcement activated
- [ ] Team engages all day

### Week +1 to +4: Post-Launch
- [ ] Onboarding sequence active
- [ ] Follow-up emails to engaged prospects
- [ ] Comparison pages published
- [ ] Roundup email includes launch
- [ ] Post-launch retrospective
