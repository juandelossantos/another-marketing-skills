# AARRR Framework (Promotion Flywheel)

## Acquisition — strangers become aware
Channels: content marketing, SEO, social media, community, Product Hunt
Skills: social-copy, showcase
Outputs per quarter: 12 LinkedIn posts, 6 Twitter threads, 4 Dev.to articles, 2 videos

## Activation — aware users get first value
Channels: onboarding email, in-app guidance, documentation
Skills: email-drip (welcome sequence)
Goal: Drive user to aha moment within first 7 days

## Retention — users stay and deepen
Channels: email sequences, re-engagement campaigns, community
Skills: email-drip (nurture + re-engagement)
Goal: Monthly active users, churn <5%

## Referral — users bring more users
Channels: word-of-mouth, social sharing, community growth
Skills: social-copy (social sharing content)
Goal: NPS >50, referral signups >10% of total

## Revenue — convert and expand
Channels: pricing page, upsell emails, annual plan incentives
Skills: launch-plan (campaign timing)
Goal: Revenue growth, expansion MRR
