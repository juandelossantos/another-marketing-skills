# Budget Planning

## Method 1 — Revenue-Based
Spend 5-40% of ARR on marketing.
Formula: (New ARR target × CAC) / annual retention
Best when historical CAC data exists.

## Method 2 — Goal-Based
Reverse-engineer budget from revenue target.
Formula: [(New ARR / (ARPC × 12)) × CAC] / retention
Best for fundraising or fixed goals.

## By Stage
| Stage | Monthly budget | Focus |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Pre-seed | $0-2K/mo | Organic only |
| Seed | $5-15K/mo | Paid test budget |
| Series A | $50-150K/mo | Full channels |

Always add 10-20% experimental budget.
