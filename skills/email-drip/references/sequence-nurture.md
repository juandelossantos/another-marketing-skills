# Nurture Sequence (6-8 emails, 2-3 weeks)

Goal: Build trust, demonstrate expertise, convert leads.

## Email 1 — Deliver Lead Magnet
Send: Immediately
Subject: Here's your [resource]
Body: Deliver promised content + brief intro
CTA: Access your [resource]

## Email 2 — Expand on Topic
Send: Day 2-3
Subject: [Specific insight] from [resource]
Body: One key takeaway expanded with new insight
CTA: Read the full guide

## Email 3 — Problem Deep-Dive
Send: Day 4-5
Subject: Why [common problem] happens (and what to do)
Body: Analyze the problem from their perspective
CTA: See how [product] solves this

## Email 4 — Solution Framework
Send: Day 6-8
Subject: A framework for [desired outcome]
Body: Introduce your methodology or approach
CTA: Apply this framework

## Email 5 — Case Study
Send: Day 9-11
Subject: How [customer] achieved [metric] in [time]
Body: Real results with specific numbers
CTA: Read full case study

## Email 6 — Differentiation
Send: Day 12-14
Subject: What makes [product] different
Body: Competitor comparison + your unique approach
CTA: See the difference

## Email 7 — Objection Handler
Send: Day 15-18
Subject: "We're not ready for [product]"
Body: Address hesitation with empathy + proof
CTA: Book a demo / Start free trial

## Email 8 — Direct Offer
Send: Day 19-21
Subject: Ready to [outcome]?
Body: Clear offer with urgency/scarcity if appropriate
CTA: Get started now
