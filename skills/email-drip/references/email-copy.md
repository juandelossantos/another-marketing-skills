# Email Copy Guidelines

## Structure per email
```
Subject: [40-60 chars]
Preview text: [90-140 chars, extends subject]
---
Hook: First line — grabs attention (1 sentence)
Context: Why this matters to them (1-2 paragraphs)
Value: The useful content (150-300 words)
CTA: One clear action (button preferred)
Sign-off: Human, warm, consistent
```

## Length by type
- Transactional: 50-125 words
- Educational: 150-300 words
- Story-driven: 300-500 words

## Tone
- Conversational, not formal
- First-person (I/we) + second-person (you)
- Active voice
- Read aloud — does it sound human?

## Formatting
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- White space between sections
- Bullet points for scannability
- Bold for emphasis (sparingly)
- Mobile-first (most read on phone)

## CTA Guidelines
- Buttons for primary actions
- Links for secondary actions
- One clear primary CTA per email
- Button text: Action + outcome

## Preview text
- 90-140 characters
- Don't repeat subject line
- Complete the thought or add intrigue
