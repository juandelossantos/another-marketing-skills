# Re-engagement Sequence (3-4 emails, 2 weeks)

Trigger: 30-60 days of inactivity.
Goal: Win back or clean list.

## Email 1 — Genuine Check-in
Send: Immediately after trigger
Subject: We miss you, [name]
Body: Acknowledge absence without guilt + what they missed
CTA: Come back / Tell us what changed

## Email 2 — Value Reminder
Send: Day 3-4
Subject: What's new since you left
Body: Best content/features/updates since last visit
CTA: See what's new

## Email 3 — Incentive
Send: Day 7-8
Subject: A special offer for you
Body: Exclusive discount, bonus, or extended trial
CTA: Claim your offer

## Email 4 — Last Chance
Send: Day 12-14
Subject: Should we stay in touch?
Body: Option to stay, snooze, or unsubscribe (clean)
CTA: Keep me updated / Unsubscribe
