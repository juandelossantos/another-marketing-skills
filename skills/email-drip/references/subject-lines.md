# Subject Line Formulas

Source: CXL research (150k+ headlines tested), Sumo, Unbounce.

## 15 Proven Formulas

| # | Formula | Example | Best for |
|---|---------|---------|----------|
| 1 | Curiosity Gap | "The surprising reason most..." | Welcome, Nurture |
| 2 | Direct Benefit | "5 ways to [outcome]" | Onboarding |
| 3 | How To | "How to [do X] in [Y time]" | Nurture, Welcome |
| 4 | Personalized | "[Name], quick question" | All |
| 5 | Social Proof | "How [customer] got [result]" | Nurture, Post-purchase |
| 6 | Urgency | "Only [X] spots remaining" | Re-engagement |
| 7 | Loss Aversion | "Stop losing [X] to [Y]" | Nurture |
| 8 | Question | "Are you making [X] mistake?" | Welcome, Nurture |
| 9 | Numbers/Data | "[X] stats that change how you..." | Nurture |
| 10 | Contrarian | "Everything you know about [X] is wrong" | Nurture |
| 11 | Intriguing | "I was wrong about [X]" | Welcome |
| 12 | Exclusive | "Exclusive: [insight]" | Re-engagement |
| 13 | Promise | "Your [goal] starts here" | Welcome |
| 14 | Story | "I tried [X] for [timeframe]" | Welcome, Nurture |
| 15 | Humorous | "Don't open this email" | Re-engagement |

## Best Practices (CXL)
- Keep under 45 characters (60 max)
- First 25 characters most critical on mobile
- Personalization in first 25 chars → ~40% lift
- 1 exclamation mark max
- Avoid ALL CAPS (triggers spam filters)
- Emojis: neutral B2B, slight lift B2C if on-brand
