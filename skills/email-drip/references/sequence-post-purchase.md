# Post-Purchase Sequence (3-5 emails, 10 days)

Goal: Validate purchase decision, upsell, drive referral.

## Email 1 — Thank You + Confirmation
Send: Immediately after purchase
Subject: You're in! Here's what's next
Body: Confirmation + what to expect + support info
CTA: Access your purchase

## Email 2 — Getting Started
Send: Day 1-2
Subject: Your first week with [product]
Body: Quick start guide + pro tips
CTA: Start here

## Email 3 — Social Proof
Send: Day 3-5
Subject: You're in good company
Body: Customer success stories + community
CTA: Join the community

## Email 4 — Upsell / Expand
Send: Day 6-8
Subject: [Product] works even better with [feature/plan]
Body: Related feature or plan upgrade
CTA: Explore [upsell]

## Email 5 — Referral
Send: Day 9-10
Subject: Share the love (get [reward])
Body: Referral program + incentive
CTA: Refer a friend
