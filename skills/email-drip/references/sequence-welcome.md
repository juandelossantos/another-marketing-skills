# Welcome Sequence (5-7 emails, 12-14 days)

Goal: Activate new signups, build trust, drive first conversion.

## Email 1 — Welcome + Deliver Value
Send: Immediately after signup
Subject: Welcome to [product]! Here's your [value]
Body: Thank + deliver promised value + set expectations
CTA: Access your [resource] / Start [first action]

## Email 2 — Quick Win
Send: Day 1-2
Subject: Get the most out of [product] in 5 minutes
Body: Show one quick action that delivers immediate value
CTA: Try [specific feature]

## Email 3 — Story/Why
Send: Day 3-4
Subject: The reason we built [product]
Body: Founder story, origin, mission — human connection
CTA: Reply and tell us about you

## Email 4 — Social Proof
Send: Day 5-6
Subject: How [customer] achieved [result] with [product]
Body: Case study or testimonial — specific numbers
CTA: See how they did it

## Email 5 — Overcome Objection
Send: Day 7-8
Subject: "But what if [common objection]?"
Body: Address top objection with empathy + proof
CTA: Start your [trial/plan]

## Email 6 — Core Feature Highlight
Send: Day 9-11
Subject: The feature our users love most
Body: Deep dive into best feature — show, don't tell
CTA: Try [feature]

## Email 7 — Conversion
Send: Day 12-14
Subject: Your [product] journey is just beginning
Body: Recap value delivered + what's next + upgrade CTA
CTA: Upgrade to [plan] / Continue free
