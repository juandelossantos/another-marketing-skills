# Confidence Rubric

Every insight must be labeled with confidence before presentation.

## Levels

| Confidence | Criteria | Action |
|------------|----------|--------|
| **High** | Theme appears in 3+ independent sources, mentioned unprompted, consistent across segments | Safe to use in messaging |
| **Medium** | Theme appears in 2 sources, or only when prompted, or limited to one segment | Use with caveat, validate further |
| **Low** | Single source, may be outlier, needs validation | Flag as hypothesis, do not base messaging on it |

## Bias Checks

| Source | Bias | Mitigation |
|--------|------|------------|
| G2/Capterra reviews | Skew toward power users + strong opinions | Cross-reference with support tickets |
| Support tickets | Skew toward problems, not value | Balance with win/loss interviews |
| Reddit | Skews technical + skeptical | Compare with review site sentiment |
| App Store reviews | Skew toward extreme experiences | Read the middle (3-star) |
| User interviews | Social desirability bias | Look for what they DO, not what they SAY |

## Minimum Viable Sample

- **Personas:** Do not create from fewer than 5 independent data points per segment
- **Messaging themes:** Minimum 3 sources before recommending
- **Competitive insights:** Cross-reference competitor reviews + community mentions

## Recency

- Weight sources from last 12 months more heavily
- A 3-year-old transcript may reflect a different product and buyer
- If all sources are >12 months old, flag as potentially stale
