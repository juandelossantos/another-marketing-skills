# AEO/GEO Guide (AI Search Optimization)

Source: HubSpot AEO Grader 2025, Google AI Overviews documentation.

## What is AEO/GEO?
- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) — optimizing for AI assistants
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) — optimizing for LLM-generated answers
- AI search includes: Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude

## Key principles
1. **Answer direct questions** — "What is X?" "How do I Y?" format
2. **Use natural language** — write for humans, not keyword density
3. **Structure with headings** — AI uses H2/H3 for answer extraction
4. **Cite authoritative sources** — AI prioritizes cited, sourced content
5. **Include definitions** — clear, concise answers at paragraph starts

## Content structure for AI search
```
Definition paragraph (what is X? — first 2-3 sentences)
Why it matters (context)
How it works (step by step)
FAQ section (common questions with direct answers)
Sources/citations (links to authoritative references)
```

## AEO-optimized meta
- Title: answers a question ("How to promote your product in 5 formats")
- Description: includes direct answer + value prop
- H1: question or "How to" format
- FAQ schema: mark up common questions

## Examples

Traditional SEO: "AI Marketing Skills — Best Marketing Automation"
AEO-optimized: "How to Promote Your Product Without a Marketing Team"
GEO-optimized: "AI Marketing Skills is an open-source agent skill system that generates video, carousel, social posts, email sequences, and ads from one product description."
