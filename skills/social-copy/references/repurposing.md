# Content Repurposing System

Turn one piece of pillar content into 5-8 social posts.

## Blog post → Social
| Output | Format |
|--------|--------|
| LinkedIn | Key insight + link in comments |
| LinkedIn carousel | 10 slides of main points |
| Twitter thread | 5-10 tweet takeaways |
| Instagram carousel | Visual summary of key idea |
| Reel | 30s video summarizing the post |
| Dev.to article | Cross-post with canonical URL |

## Podcast/video → Social
1. Transcribe (Whisper, Descript)
2. Mark 5-10 best moments
3. Extract clips (15-60s each)
4. Write standalone caption per clip
5. Add subtitles
6. Schedule across 1-2 weeks

## Newsletter → Social
| Extract | Output |
|---------|--------|
| Main insight | LinkedIn post |
| Curated links | Twitter thread |
| Stat/data | Quote graphic |
| Hot take | Twitter/LinkedIn post |

## Per pillar content, aim for:
- 1 LinkedIn post + 1 carousel
- 1 Twitter thread (5-10 tweets)
- 1 Instagram carousel or Reel
- 1 Dev.to article (if technical)
- 1 newsletter section (if applicable)
