# Content Calendar & Batching

## Weekly template

| Day | LinkedIn | Twitter | Instagram | Dev.to |
|-----|----------|---------|-----------|--------|
| Mon | Industry insight | Thread | Carousel | — |
| Tue | Behind-scenes | Hot take | Reel | — |
| Wed | Educational | Thread | Educational | Article |
| Thu | Story post | Engagement | Story | — |
| Fri | Hot take | Weekend prep | Reel | — |

## Batching strategy (2-3 hrs/week)
1. Review content pillars (what's the theme this week?)
2. Write 5 LinkedIn posts
3. Write 3 Twitter threads + daily tweets
4. Create 1 Instagram carousel + 2 Reel ideas
5. Draft 1 Dev.to article
6. Schedule everything
7. Leave room for real-time engagement

## Posting benchmarks
- LinkedIn: 3-5x/week, carousels best
- Twitter: 3-4x/day, threads best
- Instagram: 3-5x/week, Reels 2x reach
- TikTok: 2-5x/week, 15-30s
- Dev.to: 1-2x/week, 4-7 min read
