# Facebook Platform Guide

**Best for:** Communities, local business, older demographics
**Optimal frequency:** 1-2x/day
**Best format:** Images (5.20% engagement), Video (4.84%)

## What works
- Facebook Groups (community building)
- Native video (uploaded directly, not linked)
- Live video
- Local content and events
- Discussion-prompting questions

## What doesn't
- Links to external sites (algorithm penalizes)
- Pure promotional content
- Cross-posting without adaptation
