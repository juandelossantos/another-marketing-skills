# LinkedIn Platform Guide

**Best for:** B2B, thought leadership, professional networking
**Optimal frequency:** 3-5x/week
**Best format:** Carousels (21.77% engagement), Video (7.35%)
**Character limit:** 3000 per post, 1st line visible before "see more"

## What works
- Personal stories with business lessons
- Contrarian takes on industry topics
- Behind-the-scenes of building
- Original data and insights
- Carousel posts (PDF document format)
- Polls that spark discussion

## What doesn't
- Overly promotional content
- Generic motivational quotes
- Links in main post (kills reach — put in comments)
- Corporate speak without personality

## Structure
```
Hook (first 2 lines — MUST grab before "see more")
Empty line
Story/context (2-3 paragraphs)
Empty line
Insight/lesson
Empty line
CTA (question, link in comments, or discussion prompt)
```

## Algorithm tips
- First hour engagement matters most
- Comments > reactions > clicks in algorithm weight
- Dwell time signals quality
- Tag people sparingly and genuinely
