# TikTok Platform Guide

**Best for:** Brand awareness, younger audiences (16-34)
**Optimal frequency:** 2-5x/week
**Optimal length:** 15-30 seconds
**Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920)

## What works
- Native, unpolished content
- Trending sounds and formats
- Educational in entertaining wrapper
- POV and day-in-the-life
- Duets and stitches

## Video structure
```
0-2s: Hook (visual + text overlay + audio hook)
2-6s: Setup/context
6-20s: Value/payoff
20-30s: CTA (follow, comment, save)
```

## Caption best practices
- Write 150+ words blog-style (TikTok SEO)
- Include keywords naturally
- First 2-3 lines visible without "more"
- 3-5 relevant hashtags

## Text overlay principles
- 2-4 words per frame
- High contrast (white text + black stroke)
- Place in safe zone (center 2/3 of screen)
- Animate word-by-word for retention
