# Hook Formulas — 25+ Proven Patterns

Sources: CXL, Unbounce, Sumo (150k+ headlines tested), Buffer, marketing skills

## Curiosity (best for: LinkedIn, Twitter)
1. "I was wrong about [common belief]."
2. "The real reason [outcome] happens isn't what you think."
3. "[Number] years ago, I thought [X]. I was wrong."
4. "What if everything you know about [topic] is wrong?"
5. "I tried [approach] for [time] and here's what happened."

## Story (best for: LinkedIn, Instagram, Dev.to)
6. "Last week, [unexpected] happened."
7. "I almost [big mistake]. Here's what saved me."
8. "[Number] years ago, I [past state]. Today, [current state]."
9. "I was debugging [problem] for [time]. The fix? [unexpected solution]."
10. "Here's exactly what happened when I [action]."

## Value (best for: LinkedIn, Twitter, Dev.to)
11. "How to [desired outcome] without [common pain]:"
12. "[Number] [things] that [outcome]:"
13. "Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead:"
14. "The complete guide to [topic] in [time]."
15. "[Number] lessons from [experience]."

## Contrarian (best for: LinkedIn, Twitter)
16. "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]"
17. "[Common advice] is wrong. Here's why:"
18. "I stopped [common practice] and [positive result]."
19. "Why I don't [common practice]."

## Data (best for: LinkedIn, Dev.to)
20. "[X]% of [audience] do [Y]. Here's why that's wrong."
21. "We analyzed [number] [data points] and found [insight]."
22. "[Number] → [number] in [time]. Here's exactly how."

## Question (best for: LinkedIn, Twitter)
23. "Are you making this [common] mistake?"
24. "What's the one [thing] you wish you knew about [topic]?"
25. "How often do you [action] without [result]?"
