# Customer Language Extraction Guide

How to capture Voice of Customer (VoC) for `.agents/product-marketing.md`.

## Sources for VoC

1. **Sales call transcripts** — richest source
2. **Support tickets** — friction points and frustrations
3. **G2/Trustpilot reviews** — 3-star (balanced) and 5-star (emotional)
4. **Reddit** (relevant subreddits) — unfiltered customer language
5. **Social media DMs and comments**
6. **Amazon reviews** — "most helpful" reviews contain gold

## Extraction Questions

Use 3-5 questions, dig deep with follow-ups:

```
Q: "Why did you start looking for [product category]?"
  → Follow-up: "What happened right before that made you think 'I need this'?"

Q: "What was the old way you were handling this?"
  → Follow-up: "What specifically frustrated you about that?"

Q: "What does 'good' look like for you?"
  → Follow-up: "What would have to be true for you to feel this was a success?"

Q: "Tell me about the first time you realized it was working"
  → Follow-up: "What specifically changed?"
```

## How to Use VoC in Copy

- Customer quotes as testimonial headers
- Objections framed in customer language
- Pain points described using their exact words
- Benefits framed around outcomes they named
